My story

I'm a Virginia Commonwealth University 2019 alumni who earned a bachelor's degree in Painting & Printmaking. I have several years of experience working in customer service for small and large businesses, where I specialized in content marketing.

After some time in this field, I wanted to focus more on the design component of my career, and it felt natural to move into user interface design. I received my UI design certification from CareerFoundry in 2022.

My colleagues would describe me as a positive, detail-oriented individual with a passion for creativity. By incorporating both my marketing specialty and fine art background, I aim to build excellent, beautifully designed products and create positive customer experiences.
