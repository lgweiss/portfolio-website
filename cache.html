Cache: A Responsive Web App

Budgeting is complicated, paired with the difficulty of finding a company you can trust with your finances. Cache is a thoughtfully designed, easy to use responsive web app where users can save for purchases that matter to them. This app is for young to middle-aged adults who are not new to technology but do not like to work with their finances.

My Process

User Flow

I began my initial design by brainstorming the basic functions users would need for the app. I came up with a user flow diagram to determine the best way to navigate the app.

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

After creating my user flow diagram, I began to brainstorm the format of my screens. I rapidly sketched my ideas and created low-fidelity wireframes.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

I used my finalized low-fidelity wireframe sketches to create a series of mid-fidelity wireframes in Adobe XD.

Usability Testing

I tested the usability of my designs on 6 potential users, which helped me identify points of confusion in the design. Afterwards, I used Jakob Nielsenâ€™s Severity Scale the rate the errors.

Style Guide

Using the results from my usability test, I created a style guide I could follow while designing the final high-fidelity wireframes for the app.

Final Mockups

I applied my style guide to my mid-fidelity wireframes to create polished high-fidelity wireframes. I organized the wireframes to create beautiful, professionally designed mockups.

Conclusion

The final Cache app is a result of several months of work, research, and design. It is a platform that is reliable, trustworthy, and makes budgeting simple and accessible. Whether at home or on the go, anyone can save with Cache!
